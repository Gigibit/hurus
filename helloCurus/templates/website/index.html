Curus and the Environment

Happycurus is not interested in making big profits. We donate the 50% of our profits to the Planet.

Every single little green space on the Earth is the effect of the pulsing life that permeates our planet. Earth’s flourishing beauty is unique. Life in every conceivable shape and size pervades our Planet. And it also sustains it.
Human activity is altering the balance of the Earth. Forests, Oceans, Wildlife population sizes, whole Ecosystems are suffering human’s neglectful presence. The effects of our actions are already having alarming consequences, such as global warming, melting glaciers and draining of drinking water supplies. This is putting many people’s lives and homes at serious risk.
The Greenland and Antarctic ice sheets have decreased in mass. Data from NASA's Gravity Recovery and Climate Experiment show Greenland lost an average of 286 billion tons of ice per year between 1993 and 2016, while Antarctica lost about 127 billion tons of ice per year during the same time period.
Trees benefit the Planet in plenty of ways: they reduce carbon dioxide, they produce oxygen, they reduce the level of ozone, they fight against sole erosion, create ecosystems, they give food and much more.
